## 📖 Overview

Project APEX is a production-inspired analytics platform that simulates how a modern fintech company builds and scales its internal data ecosystem.

Instead of focusing on isolated dashboards or standalone machine learning models, this project develops an end-to-end analytics platform supporting:

- Product Analytics
- Growth Analytics
- Revenue Analytics
- Risk & Fraud Analytics
- Experimentation (A/B Testing)
- Analytics Engineering
- Data Engineering
- Machine Learning
- AI-powered Analytics

The platform is built around **Apex Pay**, a fictional multinational fintech company operating across India, Singapore, and the UAE.

The objective is to demonstrate production-grade analytics, engineering, and AI practices inspired by leading global fintech companies.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Modern fintech companies process millions of transactions and user interactions every day.

Every payment, login, reward redemption, loan application, fraud alert, customer support interaction, marketing campaign, and investment activity generates valuable operational data.

Transforming this data into reliable business insights requires much more than dashboards. It requires:

- Well-designed data models
- Scalable analytics pipelines
- Reliable business metrics
- Product experimentation
- Machine learning
- AI-assisted decision making

Most portfolio projects demonstrate a single dashboard or a machine learning model.

**Project APEX takes a different approach.**

It simulates the complete analytics platform behind a high-growth fintech company, following production-inspired engineering practices used by modern technology organizations.
